Jumiang Beach: Where Cliffs Meet the Sea

Experience the stunning beauty of Jumiang Beach, where pristine white sands meet majestic limestone cliffs. It's the perfect destination to unwind, enjoy the coastal scenery, and capture breathtaking photos. The gentle sea breeze and the sound of waves create a serene atmosphere, making it a favorite escape for both locals and tourists seeking tranquility.

The Enigma of the Eternal Flame

Witness a rare natural phenomenon at Api Tak Kunjung Padam. This incredible eternal flame emerges from the earth and continues to burn even when doused with water, offering a truly magical and unforgettable sight that has puzzled visitors for generations.

A View from Puncak Ratu

Escape to the highlands at Puncak Ratu (Queen's Peak), a scenic viewpoint 200 meters above sea level. Enjoy panoramic views of the lush landscape, relax at the uniquely designed caf√©, and find the perfect selfie spot against a backdrop of rolling hills.

The Historic Arek Lancor Monument

A historic monument symbolizing the struggle of the Madurese people. Located in the heart of Pamekasan, this landmark is a proud reminder of the region's spirit, surrounded by the city's vibrant daily life and historical significance.

Kampung Batik Klampar: A Living Heritage

Step into a village where artistry is woven into the fabric of daily life. Kampung Batik Klampar is home to hundreds of artisans who continue a generations-old tradition of creating exquisite hand-drawn batik. Here, you can witness the intricate process firsthand and purchase authentic Madurese textiles directly from the makers.
